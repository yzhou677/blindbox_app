Introduction

Shelfy is a collectible collection and marketplace companion app. We built it for collectors who want to browse catalog releases, track what they own, and explore market listings—without turning your shelf into a social network or a data product.

This policy explains what information Shelfy handles, where it stays, and what leaves your device.

Information we do not collect

Shelfy does not require an account. We do not collect:

  • Name, email, or phone number through the app
  • Precise location
  • Contacts, calendar, or SMS
  • Health, financial, or government ID information
  • User-to-user messages (no social messaging)

Information stored on your device

Collection data (local only)

Series, ownership status, wishlist marks, and custom entries are saved locally on your phone or tablet. We do not upload your shelf to Firebase or other Shelfy-operated servers.

Uninstalling Shelfy or clearing app data typically removes this information.

Photos you choose (local only)

If you attach a photo to a custom item, it is stored on your device. Personal shelf photos are not uploaded to Firebase Storage.

On-device caches

Catalog images and bundle data may be cached locally for faster offline browsing. Caches can be cleared with app storage or uninstall.

Information loaded from the internet

Firebase (catalog reference only)

Shelfy uses Google Firebase for read-oriented catalog content:

  • Cloud Firestore — brands, IPs, series, figures, and official feed items
  • Cloud Storage — catalog artwork

Google processes network traffic under the Google Privacy Policy. No Shelfy user account is created for this.

Market browse

The Market tab may request public marketplace listing data from configured third-party services. Shelfy is not a checkout flow; purchases happen externally if you follow a link.

External links

Tapping official or market links may open your system browser or another app. Their privacy policies then apply.

Children’s privacy

Shelfy is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect children’s personal information. Contact us if you have concerns.

Data retention and deletion

Your collection and photos remain on your device until you remove them, clear app data, or uninstall. There is no per-user cloud shelf account to delete on our servers in the current app version.

Security

We use practices appropriate for a local-first app: on-device storage for personal data, HTTPS for network requests, and Firebase rules for catalog access. Keep your device updated and locked.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy and will change the Last updated date above. Material changes may appear in Google Play release notes.
